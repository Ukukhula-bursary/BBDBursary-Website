About Fund

BBD’s Bursary Programme strives to support and grow talented students working towards their degree in mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, BSc, BCom informatics and game design. The Programme is designed around creating opportunities for you to have access to the financial means necessary to compete your studies alongside hands-on exposure to real-world software development, and full access to our Research and Development team (ATC). Our ultimate goal is to help you meet inspiring, likeminded individuals, build your network, share knowledge and ease your transition from student to working professional. At the end of your studies, BBD Bursars have the opportunity to join our exciting Grad Programme where you’ll dive into your career, working on real project teams and quickly upskill within BBD’s unique company culture.
